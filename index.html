Our Story

A Heavenly Experience in Every Bite

Heaven Dine is Palakkad's beloved destination for North Indian and Arabic cuisine, where the aroma of freshly prepared biryani drifts through a warm and welcoming dining room.

Nestled on Calicut Bypass Road near Trinity Eye Hospital, we've become a word-of-mouth favourite — bustling during lunch, vibrant in the evenings, and always alive with the joy of great food shared among family and friends.

From slow-cooked biryanis to golden kebabs and rich curries, every dish is a tribute to culinary craft and generous hospitality.
